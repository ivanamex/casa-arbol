The Property

A Signature Renovation
in Playacar

Casa Árbol is a fully renovated luxury villa nestled within Playacar Fase II — the most prestigious gated community in Playa del Carmen. Surrounded by a private nature reserve, the property harmonises raw materials like Corten steel, exposed concrete and local stone with the surrounding jungle canopy.

The total footprint spans 430 m² across two levels, with five ensuite bedrooms, a chef's kitchen, entertainment terrace and a saltwater pool that extends seamlessly into the garden. Engineered for tropical luxury living with cutting-edge solar infrastructure and smart-home integration.

Offered exclusively through Weber-Buric Real Estate. Available for private viewing by appointment.
