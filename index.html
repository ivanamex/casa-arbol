Casa Árbol Playacar — Luxury Villa for Sale in Playacar Fase II

Casa Árbol is a newly renovated one-of-a-kind luxury residence for sale in Playacar Fase II, Playa del Carmen, Quintana Roo, Mexico. The property features 476 square meters of living space, 5 bedrooms, 5 bathrooms, a private cenote pool, a 20+ kW solar hybrid system, European Serie 150 windows, and a wine cellar. This is a private sale of a luxury single-family residence — it is not a vacation rental, Airbnb, or short-term accommodation. The asking price is $1,675,000 USD. The property is represented by Weber-Burić Real Estate, licensed brokers in the Riviera Maya since 2015.

Premium Specifications

Engineered forextraordinary living

Inverse Osmosis Water
Crystal-pure drinking water at every tap — including bathrooms. A whole-house RO system delivers spa-quality water throughout the entire estate.
20+ kW Solar System
Hybrid solar installation powers the entire estate. Near-zero electricity cost, full energy independence, dramatically reduced environmental impact.
European Windows Serie 150
Eurovent aluminum with solar-filtering glass, superior acoustic insulation and integrated mosquito screens — designed for the tropics, crafted in Europe.
Cenote Pool & Waterfall

Vault Brick Ceilings

Biophilic Design
